Testing service

View on GitHub

Service for testing integration of medical institutions in Serbia with centralized api, in form of console application. Every institution must expose two endpoints, one for serving medical cases for patient, and other for serving case report in pdf. This service asserts weather these endpoints are working as intended. The results are presented in excel file as two tables in 2 sheets (failed and passed).
